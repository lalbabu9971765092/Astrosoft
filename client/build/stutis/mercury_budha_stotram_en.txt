Utpanna-murti-mithuna-makarasthe sama-sthite,
Priyangu-kalika-shyamam rupenapratimam budham.
Saumyam saumya-gunopetam tam budham pranamamyaham,
Puranoktam idam stotram sarva-vyadhi-vinashanam.
Sarva-kama-pradam nrityam-dhanam-dhanya-vivardhanam,
Yah pathet-shrinuya-dvapi tasya sarvam-prasidhyati.
Budha-vare-thava pratah kale yah satatam japan,
Labhate vanchitam sarvam-ekavimshati-varshakam.
Ashtottara-shatam kritva nityam-yah prayatah puman,
Tasyakhilani-siddhyanti karyani na-atra sanshayah.
