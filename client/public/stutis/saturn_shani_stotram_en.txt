Nilanjana-samabhasam ravi-putram yamagrajam,
Chhaya-martanda-sambhutam tam namami shanaishcharam.
Yah pathate shani-stotram-pratyaham bhaktito narah,
Na tasya jayate pida janma-rashi-gata-sthitah.
Yah shani-vasare stotram-etat-kuryat-prayatnatah,
Sa sarva-dukha-nirmuktah sarvan-kaman-avapnuyat.
Asya shani-stotrasya nityam patha-matrena manavah,
Muchyate sarva-pidabhyo na-atra karya vicharana.
