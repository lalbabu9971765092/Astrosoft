Hima-kunda-mrinalabham daityanam paramam gurum,
Sarva-shastra-pravaktaram bhargavam pranamamyaham.
Asya shukra-stotrasya pathat-siddhyanti karyani,
Dhruvam na-atra na sandehah sarva-kama-phalam labhet.
Yah pathet-ekadashakam stotram-bhaktya tu shukra-vasare,
Sa sarvan-kaman-avapnoti na-atra karya vicharana.
Stotram yah pathate martyah pratyaham-bhakti-samyutah,
Tasya-akhilani-siddhyanti karyani na-atra sanshayah.
