**Doha**
Shri Guru Charan Saroj Raj, Nij Man Mukur Sudhari.
Barnau Raghuvar Bimal Jasu, Jo Dayaku Phal Chari.
Buddhiheen Tanu Janike, Sumirau Pavan-Kumar.
Bal Buddhi Vidya Dehu Mohi, Harahu Kalesh Bikaar.

**Chaupai**
Jai Hanuman Gyan Gun Sagar.
Jai Kapis Tihun Lok Ujagar॥1॥

Ram Doot Atulit Bal Dhama.
Anjani Putra Pavansut Nama॥2॥

Mahabir Vikram Bajrangi.
Kumati Nivar Sumati Ke Sangi॥3॥

Kanchan Baran Biraj Subesa.
Kaanan Kundal Kunchit Kesa॥4॥

Haath Bajra Au Dhwaja Biraje.
Kandhe Moonj Janeu Saje॥5॥

Shankar Suvan Kesari Nandan.
Tej Pratap Maha Jag Vandan॥6॥

Vidyavan Guni Ati Chatur.
Ram Kaj Karibe Ko Atur॥7॥

Prabhu Charitra Sunibe Ko Rasiya.
Ram Lakhan Sita Man Basiya॥8॥

Sukshma Roop Dhari Siyahi Dikhawa.
Bikat Roop Dhari Lanka Jarawa॥9॥

Bhim Roop Dhari Asur Sanhare.
Ramchandra Ke Kaj Sanvare॥10॥

Lay Sanjivan Lakhan Jiyaye.
Shri Raghuvir Harashi Ur Laye॥11॥

Raghupati Kinhi Bahut Badai.
Tum Mam Priya Bharat-hi Sam Bhai॥12॥

Sahas Badan Tumhro Jas Gavain.
As Kahi Shripati Kanth Lagavain॥13॥

Sankadik Brahmadi Munisa.
Narad Sharad Sahit Ahisa॥14॥

Jam Kuber Digpal Jahan Te.
Kavi Kovid Kahi Sake Kahan Te॥15॥

Tum Upkar Sugreevahi Kinha.
Ram Milay Raj Pad Dinha॥16॥

Tumhro Mantra Vibhishan Mana.
Lankeshwar Bhaye Sab Jag Jana॥17॥

Jug Sahastra Jojan Par Bhanu.
Lilyo Tahi Madhur Phal Janu॥18॥

Prabhu Mudrika Meli Mukh Mahi.
Jaladhi Langhi Gaye Acharaj Nahi॥19॥

Durgam Kaj Jagat Ke Jete.
Sugam Anugrah Tumhre Tete॥20॥

Ram Duare Tum Rakhvare.
Hot Na Aagya Binu Paisare॥21॥

Sab Sukh Lahain Tumhari Sarna.
Tum Rakshak Kahu Ko Dar Na॥22॥

Aapan Tej Samharo Apai.
Teeno Lok Hank Te Kampai॥23॥

Bhoot Pishach Nikat Nahi Avai.
Mahabir Jab Naam Sunavai॥24॥

Nasai Rog Harai Sab Pira.
Japat Nirantar Hanumat Bira॥25॥

Sankat Te Hanuman Chudavai.
Man Kram Vachan Dhyan Jo Lavai॥26॥

Sab Par Ram Tapasvi Raja.
Tin Ke Kaj Sakal Tum Saja॥27॥

Aur Manorath Jo Koi Lavai.
Soi Amit Jivan Phal Pavai॥28॥

Charo Jug Partap Tumhara.
Hai Parsiddh Jagat Ujiyara॥29॥

Sadhu Sant Ke Tum Rakhvare.
Asur Nikandan Ram Dulare॥30॥

Asht Siddhi Nau Nidhi Ke Data.
As Bar Din Janaki Mata॥31॥

Ram Rasayan Tumhre Pasa.
Sada Raho Raghupati Ke Dasa॥32॥

Tumhre Bhajan Ram Ko Pavai.
Janam Janam Ke Dukh Bisravai॥33॥

Ant Kaal Raghuvarpur Jai.
Jahan Janm Haribhakt Kahai॥34॥

Aur Devta Chitt Na Dharai.
Hanumat Sei Sarv Sukh Karai॥35॥

Sankat Katai Mitai Sab Pira.
Jo Sumirai Hanumat Balbira॥36॥

Jai Jai Jai Hanuman Gosain.
Kripa Karahu Gurudev Ki Nai॥37॥

Jo Sat Bar Path Kar Koi.
Chhutahi Bandi Maha Sukh Hoi॥38॥

Jo Yah Padhai Hanuman Chalisa.
Hoy Siddhi Sakhi Gaurisa॥39॥

Tulsidas Sada Hari Chera.
Kijai Nath Hriday Mah Dera॥40॥

**Doha**
Pavan Tanay Sankat Haran, Mangal Murati Roop.
